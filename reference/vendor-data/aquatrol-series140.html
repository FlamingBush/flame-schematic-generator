Series 140 Safety Valves

Compact Air/Gas Safety

Includes: 140, 141, 143
↑ Air/Gas

Overview

The Series 140 valve line is designed for air/gas safety. Compact in nature, yet rugged in all aspects of design and construction. The 140 series offers excellent fast acting reseating that minimizes loss of medium. High capacity flow rates.

ASME and National Board Certified for Section XIII “UV” for Section VIII Div. 1 air and inert gas services.

Usages

  • Compressors, Air and Gas receivers
  • Gas lines, Steam lines
  • Piping system/tanks
  • Steam cleaners, Sterilizers, Autoclaves

Important: AIR AND INERT GAS ONLY – No steam, no liquid.

Specifications

Pressure Range

0-350 psig (140), 0-450 psig (141/143)

Temperature Range

-20°F to 406°F (140/141), -20°F to 800°F (143)

Certifications

ASME Section VIII (air and inert gas only), National Board

Connection Types

NPT, BSPT, BSPP (1/4" to 3/4")

Materials

140: Brass body, stainless ball, brass bonnet
141: Stainless body, stainless ball, brass bonnet
143: Stainless body, stainless ball, stainless bonnet
