Product Description

The Beduan Brass Electric Solenoid Valve 1/2" 12V is a reliable and durable solution for controlling the flow of water, air, gas, fuel, and oil in a wide variety of applications. Designed for performance and longevity, this normally closed valve opens only when energized, ensuring safe and precise control of your system’s media. When power is off, the valve remains closed, preventing unwanted flow and enhancing overall system safety. Constructed from high quality brass, this solenoid valve offers excellent resistance to corrosion and wear, making it ideal for demanding environments and continuous use. The robust brass body ensures long service life and consistent performance, even under challenging operating conditions. At the heart of the valve is a premium copper solenoid coil, not aluminum. This high quality copper coil provides superior electrical conductivity, efficient operation, and improved heat dissipation. The result is stable performance, reliable actuation, and reduced risk of coil failure over time. The Beduan solenoid valve features a 1/2" female NPT port size, making it compatible with a wide range of standard piping and fittings. The 2W160-15 designation reflects a proven, industry-recognized configuration that is easy to integrate into existing systems, whether for industrial, commercial, or DIY projects. Operating on 12V, this valve is well suited for low-voltage control systems, automotive and marine applications, fluid automation projects, and many other uses where dependable on/off control is required. Its normally closed design helps conserve energy and maintain safety by activating only when necessary. Choose the Beduan Brass Electric Solenoid Valve for a combination of sturdy brass construction, a high quality copper coil, and versatile compatibility. It is engineered to deliver consistent, long-lasting performance for water, air, gas, fuel, and oil control in your system.

Questions & Answers

This valve is suitable for use with water, air, gas, fuel, and light oil, provided they are clean and free of large particles or debris.

The valve has a 1/2" female NPT port size, model designation 2W160-15, compatible with standard 1/2" NPT threaded fittings.

This is a normally closed (NC) solenoid valve, meaning it stays closed without power and opens only when the 12V coil is energized.

The valve uses a 12V electric solenoid coil. Be sure to match the power supply to 12V and correct polarity if using DC.

The valve body is made of durable brass, and it uses a high-quality copper solenoid coil, not an aluminum coil.

Yes, it can typically be installed in either horizontal or vertical orientation, but for best performance and longevity, follow standard solenoid valve installation practices and ensure proper flow direction.

The copper coil is designed for durability and can handle frequent cycling, but continuous energizing for long periods may generate heat. Ensure adequate ventilation and confirm duty cycle requirements for your application.

Common uses include automated control of water lines, air systems, low-pressure gas lines, fuel transfer, and small oil circuits in industrial, automotive, and DIY projects.

While not strictly required, using a filter or strainer upstream is strongly recommended to prevent dirt or particles from damaging the internal seals and affecting operation.
