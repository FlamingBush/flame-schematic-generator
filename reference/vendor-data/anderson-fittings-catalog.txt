CONSTRUCTION
• Two piece – body and nut
• Straights and shapes are manufactured from CA360, CA345 or CA377 brass
• 45° flare
• Heavy duty flare fittings are manufactured to SAE specifications
ADVANTAGES
• Low cost
• Available with long or short nuts
• Can be assembled and re-assembled repeatedly
• Resists mechanical pull-out
• Shapes are available as extrusions or forgings as indicated
APPLICATION
• Compatible with copper, brass, aluminum and steel hydraulic tubing that can be flared
• Used on low, medium and high pressure lines under the most adverse conditions
• Fair vibration resistance. The long nut offers greater support
• Temperature range from –65° to +250° F.
ORDERING INFORMATION
• Flare tube fittings are supplied less nuts
• Shapes which contain an “F” at the conclusion of the prefix are forged.
For example: E1F-6B is forged
